Ethiopian Yirgacheffe

Known for its bright acidity and floral notes, Ethiopian Yirgacheffe coffee is grown in the Yirgacheffe region of Ethiopia.

Colombian Supremo

Colombian Supremo coffee is characterized by its well-balanced flavor profile, medium body, and hints of nuttiness.

Brazilian Santos

Brazilian Santos coffee is known for its mild flavor, low acidity, and chocolatey undertones. It is grown in the Santos region of Brazil.

Sumatra Mandheling

Sumatra Mandheling coffee is famous for its full body, low acidity, and earthy, chocolatey flavor. It is grown in the Mandheling region of Sumatra.
